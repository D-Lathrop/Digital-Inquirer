Overview

Terra Invicta, the highly anticipated galactic strategy game set exclusively in our solar system, has taken the gaming community by storm since its release earlier this year. Developed by Pavonis Interactive, the studio behind the acclaimed Long War mod for XCOM, Terra Invicta offers players an expansive and immersive experience in the realm of interstellar politics, diplomacy, and warfare.

Gameplay

In Terra Invicta, players assume the role of a leader on Earth who must navigate the complex web of interstellar relations within our solar system. The game offers a mix of turn-based and real-time strategy elements, with a strong focus on diplomacy and decision-making. As you expand your influence beyond Earth, you'll engage in negotiations, form alliances, and, when necessary, wage epic space battles to defend the planet.
The game's depth lies in its intricate systems, allowing players to customize their approach to solar system dominance. Whether you prefer peaceful coexistence or aggressive expansion, Terra Invicta provides the tools and challenges to test your strategic acumen.

Terra Invicta has garnered attention from both gamers and critics alike.
